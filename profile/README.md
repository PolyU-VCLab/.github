## Welcome to the Visual Computing Lab at HK PolyU 👋

The **Visual Computing Lab** at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, led by [**Prof. Lei Zhang**](https://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~cslzhang/), works on image/video restoration and quality assessment, multimodal perception and reasoning, image/video synthesis, 3D perception and generation, efficient architectures and training, as well as benchmarks and datasets.

Here we share our **projects, code, models, benchmarks, datasets, and demos**.
