## Welcome to the Visual Computing Lab at HK PolyU 👋

The Visual Computing Lab at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, led by [Prof. Lei Zhang](https://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~cslzhang/), works on **Image and video restoration/generation**, **3D/Multimodal understanding**, and **Efficient architectures and training**.

Here we share our projects, code, models, benchmarks, datasets, and demos.

### 🔥 Research Areas

- 🖼️ Image and video restoration  
- 🎨 Image and video generation  
- 🌍 3D/Multimodal understanding   
- ⚡ Efficient architectures and training  

If you are interested in our work, welcome to **follow** the organization and **star** our repositories ⭐
